Software Description

Our innovative product authentication system leverages advanced AI, machine learning, and neural networks to ensure the authenticity of products, initially focusing on footwear. The system distinguishes genuine products from counterfeits through image capture and live analysis, ensuring consumer trust and brand integrity. Additionally, it includes a vendor reliability assessment tool that evaluates vendors based on sales history, past performance, and product evaluations.
